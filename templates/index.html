Get insights into Hostelworld reviews



Ever ended up in a hostel with a high rating on Hostelworld but had a terrible experience? Then you have come to the right place!

HW-Reviews aims to analyse Hostelworld hostel reviews in more detail to gain a better understanding of a hostel before visiting and identify any inaccuracies in ratings. Insights include:

  • A predicted rating of a hostel solely based on the text reviews previous visitors have left. Hostelworld use certain aspects of your stay to calculate an overall rating. Some travellers may have had a terrible experience overall but still give aspects such as location or staff a good rating which may lead to an inflated overall rating. In this case the text review is likely to be more informative than the rating
  • Reviews where the rating a visitor provided and the predicted rating based on the text they wrote differ
  • A list of positive and negative sentences visitors have left in reviews to help identify strengths and flaws of a hostel
